Help & Support

Welcome to the Help Page! Below, you will find detailed information about each feature in the Finance Diary app. Navigate through this guide to make the most out of the app's features.

Overview

The Overview page provides:

  • A To-Do List to manage daily tasks.
  • A Google Calendar integration that displays daily financial credits or debits.

Tracker

The Tracker page helps you:

  • Automatically track expenses from UPI transactions.
  • Select and add expenses to specific budgets.

Budgets

Manage your financial goals with the Budgets page:

  • Create multiple budgets.
  • Set minimum and maximum targets for each budget.
  • Track budget changes dynamically as you add expenses.

Invest

Explore personalized stock recommendations based on your financial goals and market trends to make informed investment decisions.

Sponsors

The Sponsors page introduces services and products designed to help you save money effectively and maximize value.

Contact Us

Need help? The Contact Us page includes:

  • Our office location for in-person support.
  • An immediate response contact form for quick assistance.

Budget Planner

Plan your finances like a pro using the advanced Budget Planner. Customize and optimize your budgets with powerful tools and insights.
